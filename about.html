About us

About our team

Here at Academix Hub, we aspire to help you achieve your best results at school. Our team of highly educated and experienced tutors can help you to learn and study the content for a wide range of secondary school subjects. Our program is designed to support and enhance the learning that is done in the classroom, through a range of fun and interesting activities. Content is presented to students in a clear, and creative way, to ensure you will easily understand what you are being taught. Our tutors provide the necessary support and encouragement to help you feel confident going into your tests, SACs and Exams.

Subscribe to our newsletter

Our newsletters are sent out monthly, we send VCE updates, new study guides (from VCAA), and student lectures for your subjects as well as many relevant resources that will assist you in your assessments. The resources and informaiton we provide always match the subjects specific to you so there is no need to filter through irrelevant informaiton.
